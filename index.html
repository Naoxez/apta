(SERVICES)

OUR SERVICES

We breathe life and emotions into your spaces by serving you with ingenious products and services. Choose from our various services for the best lighting solutions

(01)

LIGHTING DESIGN

Studying the interior and exterior space of a project by special software simulations that help in designing optimum lighting solutions that amp up the look and feel of the space.

(02)

MATERIAL ADVICE

We suggest and procure materials as per color scheme of the structure and the desired lighting effect demanded by the client.

(03)

LIGHTING ASSETMENT

Survey and assessment of the whole space along with proposed passage and positioning of the structures. This helps in advanced estimation of errors and how they can be rectified by creative uses of lighting design.

(04)

CUSTOMISED LIGHTNING SOLUTIONS

Apta helps builders, architects and property owners by developing new lighting designs that are specially customised to suit their spaces.

(05)

REMODELLING

Our team have shown impressive results for old structures and areas of historical importance. We perform remodelling by smart placing of lights on ideal positions and making them interact with the pieces of attraction.

(06)

DESIGN CONSULTANCY

This is Apta's fastest growing service where we work with clients from day one of foundation till the final day of project execution. Here we provide a host of designing services that help clients in preparing a Detailed Project Report (DPR)
